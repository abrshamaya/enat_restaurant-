About

Located near Landmark Plaza, Enat Restaurant will treat guests to traditional cuisine from Ethiopia with a modern flare. Enat, meaning mother or mama in Amharic the official language of Ethiopia was the owner's inspiration behind this family oriented business. Every has a certain connection with their mother and or their mother's cooking. And the saying "Mother knows Best" definitely applies with Enat Restaurant! This Restaurant and Sports Bar is a family oriented establishment, decorated with Plasma screen TVs and beautiful wood furniture, offering the best of Ethiopian cuisine.
